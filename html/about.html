MY BIOGRAPHY

MY BIOGRAPHY

EARLY LIFE

I was born on 23rd December 1998 in the city of Patna, Bihar. A significant part of my very early childhood was spent there, where I lived for close to 4 years and was brought up by my maternal family. I had spent a good amount of time with my maternal grandparents and uncles in my childhood. That was the first of the 9 cities across 6 states where I had lived within the first 20 years of my life.

After moving through various places, I moved to Raigarh, a small town in the state of Chhattisgarh in the year 2006. That was the place where I spent nearly half of my childhood. I was admitted to O.P. Jindal School in 3rd standard, and it helped me discover all the hidden potential that I possessed. This city is naturally very close to my heart, and the closest of my friends today are my friends from Raigarh. I studied there until 10th standard, post which I moved to Hyderabad for higher studies and coaching. As a result of two years of dedication and hard work, I cleared JEE Advanced with an All India Rank of 3191. I joined IIT Kharagpur and started pursuing my B.Tech. in Aerospace Engineering.

KGP DIARIES

I was admitted to IIT Kharagpur in 2016 as an undergraduate student in the Department of Aerospace Engineering. However, at the end of my first year, I changed my branch to Chemical Engineering. If Raigarh was the place where I had discovered my potential, KGP was the place that helped me develop it further. Over my four years of stay there, I participated in various co-curricular activities, competitions and got to experience startup culture well. The first two years of my stay were all about exploring the opportunities present around me. I tried my luck in robotics, publicity and marketing. I was a part of two startups as well, one in each year of stay there. All the experiences taught me various essential non-technical skills such as quick decision making, leadership as well as time management.

It was at the beginning of my third year that I started to finalise my areas of interest. A big failure early on during the internship period helped me realise my shortcomings and I started working on improving my technical skillset. It was at that moment when I found an interest in programming and software development. I also tried learning a bit of data science. Over time, I refined my programming skills and secured a summer internship with Itiviti India Private Limited as a software intern. I enhanced my skills further and ended up with a placement offer from Jaguar Land Rover for the position of Graduate Software Engineer Trainee during the on-campus placements in December 2019.

I graduated from IIT Kharagpur in June 2020. Although I hadn't demonstrated any significant interest in my core discipline, Chemical Engineering; I was serious about my studies and was presented with the prestigious silver medal for having the highest CGPA among the successfully graduating candidates in my branch.
